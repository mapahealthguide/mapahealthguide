<h3>What is All-on-4?</h3>
<p>All-on-4 is a revolutionary dental implant procedure designed for individuals who have lost most or all of their teeth. This technique involves placing four strategically positioned implants in the jawbone, which serve as a stable foundation for a full arch of replacement teeth. Unlike traditional implants, which can require multiple surgeries and a lengthy healing process, All-on-4 offers a streamlined solution that can often be completed in just a few visits.</p>

<h3>The All-on-4 Procedure</h3>
<p>The All-on-4 treatment typically involves the following steps:
1. <strong>Consultation</strong>: A thorough examination and consultation will help determine if you are a suitable candidate for the procedure.
2. <strong>Implant Placement</strong>: Under local anesthesia, the four implants are placed in the jawbone. The strategic positioning allows for maximum support and stability.
3. <strong>Temporary Prosthesis</strong>: Often, a temporary denture can be placed on the same day, allowing you to leave the clinic with a functional smile.
4. <strong>Healing and Final Restoration</strong>: After a healing period, a permanent, custom-made prosthesis is fitted, completing your smile transformation.</p>
